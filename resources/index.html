Diversity Resources

This page contains links to a series of introductory articles on Diversity, Equity and Inclusion, as well as resources for those seeking diversity-related groups and resources at Mudd and the 5Cs.

Articles on Diversity, Equity, and Inclusion

These articles are meant to provide the reader with a brief introduction to concepts surrounding diversity and provide a background so one can engage in and understand discussions. The page will be updated as more articles are written.

Campus Resources

There is a wonderful set of resources about Diversity, including learning outcomes, affinity group information, resources for transgender students, and so on from the OID website.

The DSA also maintains a Resource Guide that includes information about wellness support and 5C resources.

Mudd's "Other Resources for Students" includes links to the Office of Black Student Affairs, the Queer Resource Center, the International Place, and other helpful resources.
